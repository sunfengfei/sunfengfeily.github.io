Co-Founder of Economic Knowledge Sharing Platform

WeChat Official Account: Beyond Economics

Jan 1, 2019

In my sophomore year, my friend and I found that there were few official account in Wechat targeted for sharing economic knowledge, so with the guidance of Professor Zhou, we established an online economic papers sharing platform Beyond Economics.

We not only wanted to found an account to spread specific academic knowledge to the public, but also build the bridge of information connecting people who are devoted to academic fields of economics.

Generally, our pushes(tweets) include fields of both microeconomics and macroeconomics and involve:

  • Summary of classic dissertations
  • Summative translation articles for frontier papers

With efforts, we have accumulated over 3,500 followers and expanded into an academic group with over 50 article writing contributors. I am glad to make contributions to better access to academic resourses and higher popularity of academic discussion among university students.
